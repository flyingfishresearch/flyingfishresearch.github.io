10 Nov 2025

Huge congratulations to CWT United (Joint Team of Tongji SRIAS, CUHK, and Wuhan University) for winning the double championships (Indoor + Outdoor) at IMAV 2025!

The team dominated the International Micro Air Vehicle Competition (IMAV 2025) in Puebla, Mexico, claiming both the Indoor and Outdoor search-and-rescue championships, and was awarded an additional “Outstanding Performance” medal.

恭喜由同济大学科学中心、香港中文大学与武汉大学组成的联合团队（CWT United）荣获 IMAV 2025 室内赛与室外赛“双料冠军”，并因室内任务的出色表现额外获得 “Outstanding Performance” 特别奖牌！
